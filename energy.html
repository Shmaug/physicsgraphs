Energy is the abilty to do work and is classified into two categories, Kinetiec Energy (KE) and Potential Energy (PE).

Kinectic Energy

Kinetic energy (KE) is the the energy of motion thus, if an object is moving it has kinetic energy.

Potential Energy

Potential energy (PE) measures the energy that an object is capable of possesing. In the most baisc case, as an object is lifted higher its potential energy increases.

Mechanical energy states that in a system the sum of the kinetic energy and potential energy will always be the the same value in perfect enviroment.
